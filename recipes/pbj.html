PB & J

Description

A classic US lunch option, virtually unknown (and sometimes derided!) in other countries, the PB&J is an simple and easy filling meal.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread (in pairs of slices)

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter over one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly over the other slice of bread
  3. Place peanut butter side on jelly side
  4. Optional: Cut into wedges, halves, or remove crusts
