Dofu Stir Fry

Description

This yummy stir fry comes together quickly. It's fresh and filling, perfect for any weeknight meal. By brining the dofu, we get a nice crispy exterior and deliciously soft interior. And we can prep everything while the dofu brines, so the whole meal can be finished in 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 block medium or hard dofu
  • 2 cups long-cooking veggies
  • 2-3 cups medium-cooking veggies
  • 4 cloves garlic, 1 limb ginger
  • 1 cups leafy greens
  • 5 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 cup rice
  • Peanut oil or other high-smoke point oil
  • Red pepper, salt, pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In small pot, combine 2 cups of water and 3 tbsp salt over heat until dissolved
  2. Place dofu in small bowl, then pour hot brine over it. Let sit for 15 minutes
  3. While dofu brines, chop veggies, mince garlic and ginger
  4. In very small bowl, add 2 tbsp hoisin, 1/4 cup chicken stock
  5. In slightly larger bowl, add rest of hoisin, 2.5 cups chicken stock, and corn starch
  6. Use remaining chicken stock to cook 1 cup rice
  7. Drain dofu
  8. Heat wok with oil till smoking
  9. Add dofu, and cook until browned on all sides, ~6 minutes
  10. Add very small bowl of glaze and cook until thick, ~3 minutes
  11. Remove dofu and sauce. Rinse wok, then return to heat with more oil
  12. Add long-cooking veggies, cook for ~9 minutes, or until browned
  13. Remove, then add more oil and wait for it to smoke
  14. Add medium-cooking veggies
  15. Once soft, clear middle and add ginger and garlic
  16. Once fragrant, stir in leafy greens
  17. Once wilted, stir in dofu and long-cooking veggies
  18. Add sauce from slightly larger bowl, and cook until thickned ~3 minutes
  19. Serve over rice
